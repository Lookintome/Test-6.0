SUMMARY

Xicor, also known as Zaiko, is the hybrid clone of Son Goku and Western Supreme Kai and main antagonist of Toyble's Dragon Ball AF series. After being raised by his mother to be the ultimate warrior he is brought to Earth after Son Goku departure at the end of GT to assert his power on the remaining Z fighters and then conquer the universe with his mother.
